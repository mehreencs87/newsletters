Introducing your new, free-forever developer plan that you can use in production.
We know there are a ton of ways you may choose to use Auth0. Based on what you’ve told us about how you use the platform and how you think about pricing, we’ve made some changes that we think you’ll like. Check out our updated pricing page for all of the details but here are a few highlights:

A new, Free Developer Plan

 That includes up to 2 social providers, a database connection and up to 7,000 active users per month. Yep, you read that correctly.

A choice of Features-based Plans

 Where you can access all Auth0 features. Play around as much as you’d like - we want you to be certain you’ll use what you buy.

A 22 day trial period

For both internal (your employees) or external (your users) usage and a plan just for third-party SSO.

How Does This Affect Me?

As an existing subscriber, you have two options:

You can keep your existing subscription (domain name [tenant name]) as-is, including the same active-user quota and feature access you have now, for the same price. You don’t need to do a thing.

Switch to the new, free developer plan (or any of the other new pricing plans). To make this change, just head to your dashboard settings, click on “billing” and make your plan selection there.
