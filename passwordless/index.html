We wanted you to be the first to know - we’ve expanded our passwordless authentication feature set! You can now easily and securely set your users up with the ability to login to your web or mobile app, command-line-interface or IoT device using just their email or phone and no passwords!  
 
  Visit the ‘connections’ section of your dashboard menu and click on ‘passwordless’ to see options for enabling user login via:  
 
Email
SMS
Apple Touch ID
 
  We’ve also introduced Lock Passwordless, a free login box widget just for use with passwordless authentication.
