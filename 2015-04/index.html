Auth0 News  
  March 2015  
  Hello! We’ve been super busy here at Auth0 and wanted to share some of
what we’re up to. You can also check out our Release Notes anytime to see
the latest news (and alllllll of the details we won’t bother you with here).		  
  A New, Super-Powered Search API  
  Searching users just by name is old hat. You can now use search to find
users by any attribute collected. Access search via our API (new look coming
soon) and shortly, straight through your Auth0 dashboard. {{content}}		  
   
 
  Lock supports Twilio SMS and Apple TouchID  
  Passwordless authentication? Yes, please. Auth0’s login widget, Lock, now
supports Twilio SMS for iOS and Android, and iOS TouchID for passwordless login.		  
 
  New Pricing  
  You asked, we listened. You can now customize a pricing plan to better fit
your needs. Check it out.		  
   
 
  Sharelock.io: Securely Share Data  
  Sharelock by Auth0 is an easy and secure way to share snippets of
data under 500 characters. Use it at sharelock.io, download the app,
or deploy it within your own servers.		  
   
 
  As always, we’re here to help. Email support@auth0.com, chat with us at
chat.auth0.com or ask questions at ask.auth0.com, anytime.		  
  Happy Authenticating,  
  The Auth0 Team
