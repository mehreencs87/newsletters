News & upcoming trends
Let's Encrypt: The free certificate authority
Let's Encrypt is a free, automated, and open certificate authority developed by the Internet Security Research Group (ISRG). It provides free X.509 certificates through an automated process designed to eliminate the current complex process of manual creation, validation, signing, installation, and renewal of certificates for secure websites. Certificates are valid for 90 days, which limits damage from key compromise and mis-issuance.
Visit the official site for more information and to try it out.
